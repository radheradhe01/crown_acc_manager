Bank Transaction Category Update in Replit

To implement a category management system similar to QuickBooks, follow the steps below:

Create Category Dropdown:

Add a dropdown to the category selection menu for selecting existing categories.

Below the existing categories, provide a "Create New" option for users to create custom categories.

Create Category Form:

When "Create New" is selected, display a form with the following fields:

Category Name: Field for inputting the name of the new category.

Main Account Type: Dropdown for selecting from the following list (based on QuickBooks knowledge):

Cash and Cash Equivalents: Bank, Cash on hand, Trust accounts, Undeposited Funds

Credit Card: Credit Card

Current Assets: Accounts Receivable, Inventory Asset, Prepaid Expenses, Employee Cash Advances, Investments, Allowance for Bad Debts

Fixed Assets: Buildings, Furniture & Fixtures, Machinery & Equipment, Vehicles, Land, Accumulated Depreciation, Intangible Assets

Non-Current Assets: Goodwill, Lease Buyout, Licenses, Organizational Costs, Security Deposits, Accumulated Amortization

Current Liabilities: Accounts Payable, Credit Card, Loan Payable, Sales Tax Payable, Payroll Liabilities, Deferred Revenue, Insurance Payable, Line of Credit

Non-Current Liabilities: Notes Payable, Shareholder Notes Payable, Other Long Term Liabilities

Owner's Equity: Owner's Equity, Retained Earnings, Opening Balance Equity, Partner Contributions, Partner Distributions, Draws

Income: Service/Fee Income, Sales of Product Income, Non-Profit Income, Discounts/Refunds Given, Unapplied Cash Payment Income

COGS (Cost of Goods Sold): Cost of Labor, Supplies & Materials, Shipping, Freight & Delivery, Equipment Rental

Expenses: Advertising/Promotional, Auto, Bank Charges, Insurance, Legal & Professional Fees, Office/General Administrative Expenses, Rent and Lease, Salaries & Wages, Utilities, Travel, Dues & Subscriptions, Meals & Entertainment

Other Income: Interest Earned, Dividend Income, Other Investment Income, Tax-Exempt Interest

Other Expense: Amortization, Depreciation, Exchange Gain or Loss, Penalties & Settlements

Submit New Category:

After the user fills out the category form, submit the new category and save it to the database.

Make sure to display the newly created category in the category dropdown list.

Integrating with Bank Transactions:

Once a category is selected (or created), the user should be able to link it with specific bank transactions.

Add functionality to filter and display bank transactions by category, allowing users to assign categories to multiple transactions.

Displaying Selected Category:

After selecting a category, display the category details (Main Account Type and associated detail types) for verification before submitting the transaction
